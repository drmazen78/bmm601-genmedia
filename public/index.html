Generative AI in Multimedia

A journey through the AI tools that create images, audio, and video. Learn how they work, see real examples, and understand both the benefits and the drawbacks.

How it Works See Examples

Main Topics

Image Generation

Tools like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion create realistic or artistic images from a simple text prompt.

Audio Generation

Tools such as ElevenLabs for voice cloning and Suno for generating music from short textual descriptions.

Video Generation

Tools such as Sora and Runway produce short, high-quality video clips starting from text or a still image.

How it Works

Diffusion models, GANs, and Transformers are the engines behind these tools. They learn from millions of samples to generate new content.

About This Site

This site was prepared as part of the Multimedia Systems (BMM601) course assignment at the Syrian Virtual University, Spring 2026 semester. Its goal is to introduce visitors to Generative AI technologies and their applications in images, audio, and video, with practical examples and a clear explanation of how the underlying models work.
